Event Rules

Specific rules for each TECHSPARK 2025 event

Digital Poster Presentation

  1. Participants must register for the event online.
  2. Each participant must prepare a digital poster related to Artificial Intelligence.
  3. Posters must be original; plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  4. The poster must follow the specified format, size, and PDF file type.
  5. Participants must carry and display their college ID card during the presentation.
  6. Mobile phones are not permitted, except if allowed by coordinators for presentation purposes.
  7. Each participant will be given 15 minutes to present and explain their poster.
  8. Posters should be clear, visually appealing, and readable on a digital screen.
  9. Judgment will be based on relevance, creativity, clarity, originality, and presentation skills.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final. Contact student coordinators for assistance.

Technical Quiz

  1. Participants can register for the quiz through online registration or spot registration.
  2. The quiz time limit is 20 minutes.
  3. Registered participants must wear and display their college ID card during the competition.
  4. Participants are not allowed to switch tabs or use other applications during the quiz.
  5. Mobile phones are strictly prohibited. Participants found using mobile phones will be disqualified.
  6. The quiz will begin exactly at the scheduled time.
  7. The quiz will consist of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
  8. Once the time limit is over, answers will be auto-submitted.
  9. Results will be generated based on the highest score and the least time taken.
  10. Participants may contact the student coordinator for further assistance.

Start-Up Pitch

  1. Only one participant can present the startup idea.
  2. An abstract must be submitted before the event.
  3. PPT presentations are allowed.
  4. Participants may bring their own laptops.
  5. Pitch duration: 10 minutes presentation + 2 minutes Q&A.
  6. Ideas must be original and related to technology, innovation, or social impact.
  7. Evaluation criteria include innovation, feasibility, business model, and presentation skills.
  8. Offensive or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
  9. The judges‚Äô decision will be final.
  10. Participants must maintain discipline and follow coordinator instructions.
